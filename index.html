About Brain Code Club

Brain Code Club is revolutionizing education by combining coding, entrepreneurship, and blockchain technology. In partnership with EATschools, we provide youth aged 8-18 with the tools, knowledge, and opportunities to become the next generation of tech leaders.

EATschools Partnership

Official partnership with EATschools ecosystem, providing students with real-world learning opportunities and industry connections.

Students earn EAT Coin cryptocurrency for completing lessons, projects, and contributing to the community.
